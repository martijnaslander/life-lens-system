Layer 1 · Stacked

Synaptic Stratification

Ten levels of evidence, from intentional contact (a phone call) to pattern recognition (AI-detected co-occurrence). Every connection carries its proof.

Brain analogy: how synapses form

Layer 2 · Stacked

Theta-Myelin

Completeness as insulation. A thin synapse (name only) fires slowly. A thick synapse (name + date + location + context + photo) fires instantly.

Brain analogy: the myelin sheath around nerve fibers

Layer 3 · Stacked

Diamond Layer

Eight dimensions per synapse: temporality, directionality, valence, weight, social graph, context, decay, and hub value. Not just connected — multidimensional.

Brain analogy: the diamond-hard coating that makes synapses indestructible

Layer 4 · Stacked

Action Potential

How the network fires. Threshold, saltatory conduction, payload, excitation vs. inhibition, summation, refractory period. Six sub-mechanisms that turn a dead archive into a thinking network.

Brain analogy: the electrical pulse that makes neurons fire

Layer 5 · Transversal

Double Helix

Every synapse carries two strands: evidence (how strong is the proof?) and meaning (is this a fact, a belief, or a hypothesis?). Like DNA, they spiral through every layer. Layer 10 — human confirmation — is the helicase that opens and reseals them.

DNA analogy: two strands encoding different information, bound at base pairs

Layer 6 · Enveloping

Choroid Plexus

The immune system of the network. Filtration at the gate, nightly cleansing, immune patrol, homeostasis. Not stacked on top — wrapped around. The environment in which all other layers function.

Brain analogy: the membrane that produces cerebrospinal fluid and guards the blood-brain barrier

Core Principles

98% data, 2% AI

The AI is the translator. The data is the story. Replace the AI tomorrow — the system keeps running. The value is in the structure, not the intelligence layer.

Zero hallucinations by design

The system retrieves, never generates. Every answer comes from a database query, not from pattern completion. Every connection carries its evidence layer and its source.

The more formal your ontology, the less intelligent your AI needs to be

Well-structured data makes AI almost redundant. A simple SQL query on a clean schema outperforms a genius model on messy data. Invest in structure, not in models.

Grows in intelligence without growing in size

A new wikilink is a few bytes. The value it unlocks is unbounded. The system gets smarter by connecting existing data, not by adding more.

Human in the loop, always

Layer 10 is human confirmation. The system proposes, the human confirms. No automated decisions about meaning. The double helix cannot be read without a human helicase.
